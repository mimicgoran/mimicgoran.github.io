Hi, I'm Goran. Welcome to my portfolio

With 10+ years of experience working on engineering projects and handling all kinds of data, I’ve somehow found myself across a mix of industries including geodesy, energy, interactive mapping and even intellectual property. Not exactly planned, but definitely useful.

Working in different environments turned out to be a big advantage. It forced me to learn fast, adapt to different methodologies and approaches, and understand how things work from multiple angles.

I work with both spatial and non spatial data and like finding practical ways to solve problems. I’m always looking to improve how things are done and, thankfully, AI has become a pretty useful teammate along the way.

What really defines me is curiosity and the constant need to improve. I’m always looking for better ways to solve problems, optimize processes and expand what I know.

Over time, I’ve also learned that good work doesn’t happen in isolation. Communication, trust and strong professional relationships matter just as much as technical skills. I value working with people, keeping things clear and honest, and making sure we move in the same direction.
